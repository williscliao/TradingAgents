Conducting a fundamental assessment and peer benchmarking for **Coherent Corp. (COHR)** as of February 17, 2026.

### PART 1: DEEP DIVE TARGET ANALYSIS (COHR)

**1. Growth Quality:**
Coherent has shown a significant recovery in growth. Following the transformative merger of II-VI and Coherent, revenue dipped in FY2024 to $4.7B but has since rebounded sharply to $5.8B in FY2025 and approximately **$6.3B (TTM)**. This ~23% YoY growth indicates that the company has moved past integration hurdles and is capturing strong demand in its core segments (optical communications, lasers, and materials), likely driven by AI-related infrastructure and industrial automation.

**2. Profitability & Moat:**
*   **Gross Margin:** Stable at **36.4%**, indicating consistent pricing power in a competitive high-tech hardware space.
*   **Operating Margin:** Currently **11.8%**, which leads its primary peer group. The margin has expanded from lower single digits post-merger, suggesting significant synergy realization and improved operational scale.
*   **Moat:** Coherent maintains a wide moat through its deep vertical integration—from raw materials (SiC, crystals) to finished laser systems—making it a critical "arms dealer" for both the semiconductor and AI industries.

**3. Financial Health:**
The balance sheet is managed conservatively given the company's size.
*   **Liquidity:** A **Current Ratio of 2.25** provides ample runway for operations.
*   **Debt Profile:** Total debt of ~$3.9B against ~$8.1B in equity results in a **Debt-to-Equity ratio of 0.48**, which is healthy and significantly lower than its closest competitor, Lumentum.

**4. Earnings Quality:**
There is a notable gap between GAAP Net Income ($49M in FY25) and **Operating Cash Flow ($633M)**. This is not a "red flag" but rather a characteristic of a company with heavy acquisition-related non-cash charges ($553M in depreciation/amortization). The high OCF relative to NI suggests the company’s underlying cash engine is much more powerful than the bottom-line GAAP figures suggest.
